Our Story

Founded in 2015, Uganda Job Connect emerged from a simple observation: there was a significant disconnect between talented job seekers and employers looking for skilled professionals in Uganda.

Today, we've successfully placed over 5,000 professionals in roles across banking, healthcare, technology, agriculture, and development sectors. Our success is measured by the long-term satisfaction of both our job seekers and employer partners.
